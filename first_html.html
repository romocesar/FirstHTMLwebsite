Steps to create a Delicious Lemonade Glass

  1. Measure 2 spoonfuls of sugar and throw it in a cup
  2. Cut and squeeze 2 lemons
  3. Place some ice on your cup
  4. Add some sparkling water
  5. Pour some tequila on it :)
